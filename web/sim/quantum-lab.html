Quantum chemistry (roadmap)

Next: seed a scene from a woven Fiber, then compute real electronic structure server-side and weave the result back as verified Knits. Tracked in #214.

Read the plan ↗
From visual sim → quantum-chemistry simulator
  1. Embed the pixelPhysics quantum sims here (this page).
  2. Wire knitwebs/chemistry term-nodes → sim inputs (elements, bonds, reaction conditions from #109).
  3. Compute electronic structure server-side with RDKit + PySCF.
  4. Quantum path via Qiskit Nature / OpenFermion / PennyLane; results woven back and pulse-verified.
